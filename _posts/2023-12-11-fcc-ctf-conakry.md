> *Competing across borders with Redteam TG, at one of West Africa's serious cybersecurity forums.*

## The FCC

The **Forum de Conakry sur la Cybersécurité (FCC)** is a West African cybersecurity conference organized by **Guinea Cybersecurity (GCS)** and **ANSSI Guinée** (Guinea's National Cybersecurity Agency). It brings together government officials, experts, and academics to discuss threats, infrastructure protection, and digital policy across the sub-region.

The 2023 edition ran **December 11-12** at the Primus Hotel Kaloum in Conakry, under the theme *"Cybersecurity: The Central Role of Humans."* The CTF is one of its main competition tracks.

## Playing with Redteam TG

For this one I played with **Redteam TG**, which brings together some of the strongest cybersecurity practitioners in Togo. The bar was high and the team was serious. Good challenge.

## The result

| Phase | Result |
|---|---|
| Pre-qualification | **1st place** |
| Final | **3rd place** |

We finished first in pre-qual and held a podium position in the final.

## Context

Playing in Guinea with a cross-border team was something different. It showed that the level I had built at home was competitive outside of it. More practically, it confirmed what I already believed: good CTF performance comes from the team dynamic as much as individual skill.
