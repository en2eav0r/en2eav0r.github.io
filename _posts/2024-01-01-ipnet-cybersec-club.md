## What I Built

I founded and currently lead the **IPNET CyberSec Club**, a structured cybersecurity training program that grew from zero to **60+ active members** spanning every academic level (L1 to M2). This is not a hobby club — it is a full pedagogical ecosystem I designed, built, and operate.

To run it at scale, I took the open-source **[cyberstudent-ctf](https://github.com/cyberstudent-ctf)** bot as a foundation and significantly extended it — adding a mandatory writeup system, tiered group management, anti-cheat enforcement, milestone role progression, and admin tracking tooling.

**Tech stack:**

| Layer | Technology | Why |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| Bot Framework | Sapphire Framework + Discord.js v14 | Event-driven, modular, production-grade |
| Database | PostgreSQL + Prisma ORM | Persistent tracking, relational data integrity |
| Language | TypeScript | Type-safety across a complex codebase |
| Deployment | Docker + PM2 | 24/7 uptime, zero-downtime restarts |
| Validation | Zod | Input integrity and security |

## What Makes This Different

### Mandatory writeup system
No points are awarded without a writeup — a detailed technical report documenting the approach, tools, exploitation steps, and remediation. Every member builds a **professional security portfolio** from day one.

### Three skill tiers
Members are split into `I-Am-New`, `I-ve-One-Year-Experience`, `I-ve-More-Experience` — each receiving a calibrated challenge every Saturday. Beginners are challenged without being crushed.

### "Learn by teaching" obligation
Any member who solves a challenge must present their methodology to the group — developing technical clarity and communication skills that matter beyond CTF competitions.

### Culture, not just tooling
- **Zero-tolerance deadline policy** — writeup by the following Saturday or temporary exclusion
- **Anti-cheat system** — the bot monitors all channels and removes any flag shared outside authorized areas
- **Best Cyber Battle Woman award** — a structural choice to recognize women in cybersecurity
- **Togo's first university-run CTF** — the [IPNET Cyber Battle](/posts/ipnet-cyber-battle/)
