> *Impact Campus Trophy & Most Innovative Student Award — IPNET Open House Day, September 22, 2025.*

## The Context

On **September 22, 2025**, IPNET Institute of Technology held its **Open House Day** — a showcase event where over **50 student IT projects** were presented before a jury and the academic community.

That day, I won **two awards simultaneously**.

---

## The Awards

### 🏆 Impact Campus Trophy
> *Recognizes the initiative with the greatest campus impact.*

Awarded for organizing the **IPNET Cyber Battle** — the first CTF competition ever organized by a university in Togo. An event entirely conceived and run by students, which placed IPNET on the national cybersecurity map.

---

### 🏅 Most Innovative Student Award
> *Honors exceptional leadership and innovation on campus.*

Recognition of the full body of work accomplished: founding the **IPNET CyberSec Club**, building its technical infrastructure, designing its pedagogy, and delivering results — members who dominate national competitions and represent Togo on the international stage.
