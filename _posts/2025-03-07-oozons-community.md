## About Oozons

### OOZONS CODE CLASH 2026: February–March 2026

<img src="/assets/images/oozons-code-clash-2026.jpg" alt="Code Clash 2026" style="border-radius: 10px; width: 65%;" />

Flagship hackathon hosted on **HackerRank**: 18 finalists, in-person finale at ESGIS Avedji.

| Rank | Prize |
|------|-------|
| 1st | 75,000 FCFA + trophy |
| 2nd | 50,000 FCFA + medal |
| 3rd | 25,000 FCFA + medal |

**Partners:** ANAC-Togo · OSSAN ASUR · TCC & ESGIS Togo · MIVO AZON

The full recap of the finale was covered by **OOZONS TECH TV**:

{% include embed/youtube.html id='bFOfM5dB4bQ' %}

The video captures the day of the finale at ESGIS Avedji. The 18 finalists who made it through the HackerRank online phase competed in-person for the top prizes. The atmosphere was high-energy: participants coding under pressure in front of a crowd, judges evaluating both output and problem-solving approach. The event included a prize ceremony and remarks from the organizing team. Beyond the competition itself, the video reflects what Code Clash was really about: showing young Togolese developers what they are capable of when given a real challenge and a proper stage to compete on.
