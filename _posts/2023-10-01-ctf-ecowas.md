> *2nd in the country. 15th in the world. Only the 1st goes to the international final.*

## The Competition

The **CTF ECOWAS** is an international Capture The Flag competition organized under the umbrella of the Economic Community of West African States. It is open to teams from across the region and beyond, with a national selection stage that determines which team earns the right to represent their country at the international final.

## The Stakes

Only **one team per country** advances to the international final. There is no silver medal for the international stage: you either win your national bracket, or the competition ends there.

## The Result

I competed with my team and we finished:

- **2nd place nationally**, out of more than **30 teams** from Togo
- **15th place internationally**, out of more than **114 countries**

The team that finished 1st nationally went on to represent Togo at the international final.

## The Lesson

15th out of 114 countries is a result I am proud of. But the only number that mattered for qualification was 1st nationally, and we fell short of that by a single place.

It was a direct lesson: in high-stakes CTF competition, the margin between advancing and staying home is razor thin. That hunger carried directly into the preparation that led to winning the CTF National du Togo the following year, and going on to represent Africa at the ICC in Japan.
