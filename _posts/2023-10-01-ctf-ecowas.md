> *2nd in the country. 15th in the world. Only the 1st goes to the international final.*

## The competition

The **CTF ECOWAS** is an international Capture The Flag competition organized under the Economic Community of West African States. It runs in two stages: a national bracket first, then an international final. Only one team per country advances.

You either win your national bracket, or the competition ends there. No consolation path.

## The result

- **2nd nationally**, out of more than **30 teams** from Togo
- **15th internationally**, out of more than **114 countries**

The team that finished 1st went on to represent Togo at the international final.

## The lesson

15th out of 114 countries is a result I'm proud of. But the only number that mattered for qualification was 1st nationally, and we missed it by one place.

That stayed with me. The margin between going home and going to the finals was one team. I spent the following year making sure it wouldn't happen again, which led directly to winning the CTF National du Togo and competing at the ICC in Japan.
